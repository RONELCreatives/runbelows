Our contemporary blinds are the “urbane” and top-notch curtain-like decoration materials that complement client’s offices and homes in terms of window covering. Blinds use varieties of control systems which our experts install and train clients on how to use them effectively. Our blinds come in colors and diverse materials that are superior in their competitive markets. Our blinds are of different forms:

Roller Blinds

Our Roller blinds are identified as a stiffened fabric held by an aluminum roller. Basically, our roller blinds can be in diverse forms, from spring-loaded to adjustable with motorized or chain mechanism. They come in different colors. At RunBelows clients are always surprised by the array of choices. We have the Blackout blinds, filter blinds, light filter, Sunscreen roller blinds and Bonded Roller blinds

Austrian blinds

We have Austrian blinds for your glass windows and doors. These blinds have its constituents as rows of vertically gathered swags of fabrics. When these blinds are drawn upwards, they form a pleat of fabrics or decorating garments that give your room and office an exotic look.

    Components of Austrian Blinds
  • Headrails – can be aluminium and timber
  • Cleat – consisting of brass or nylon to hold the lift cords
  • Cord – Normally polyester

Roman Blinds

Roman blinds are uniquely identified by their noticeable patterns in the fabrics used. The patterns add a touch to the shades that fall in your room; creating a unique atmosphere that signifies peace and love.
