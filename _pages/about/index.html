Where perspectives meet design

With a mission to be acknowledged in the global marketplace as a quality-driven company which renders both interior design and building construction services, governed by work ethics and values.

RunBelows, is an internationally known company, that is well – vested in both interior design and exterior services, furniture and construction (RBVL). RunBelows Ventures is built on a solid foundation of knowledge and service that has resonated well with our customers in Ghana and across West Africa sub region for the past 12 years. Our distinct attribute as compared to our competitors lies with the exhibition of extraordinary skills and rich experiences in delivering to our clients “nouveau” designs that uniquely identifies them. We believe that beautiful and constructive design is an integral part of a human that cannot be eliminated.

RunBelows Ventures serves in the fortune of 50 mediums, small businesses, educational institutions, healthcare companies, and government entities at the local and national levels. Our facilities are located throughout the country, with its headquarters situated in Accra. Our results have been achieved through promotion of the teamwork concept among employees and the company’s wide commitment to client satisfaction. Our achievements are possible only through the active involvement of everyone in the company.

Message from the CEO

CEO

“Our Culture In Ghana And Africa Is Marked By Stupendous Artifacts And Antiques That Tell The Tales Of History, Our Background, Heritage, Occupation, Governance And Our Environment. Based On The Inspiration Obtained From The Surrounding Elements And Sudden Admiration Developed Because Of Researches Pertaining To Available Space; I Decided To Engage In Interior Designs. I Would Normally Decorate My Room With Sea Shells And Other Fascinating Materials To Bring A Harmonious And Serene Atmosphere To My Room. This Adventure Runs Into My Future Aspirations – Manifesting Into Establishing An Interior Organization And Its Subsidiary Companies That Deals With Building Construction. As, The Ceo Of Runbelows Ventures, I Believe In Upholding The Same Values That Kept Me Shaped Up: Integrity, Safety, Innovative, Aesthetic, Truthful, And Respect. The Hallmark Of Satisfactory Artwork Is Dignified By Creativity And Respect. Today, My Company Still Stands Tall Because Of Requisite Values Set And Upheld By All Employees”.

Mission

To be acknowledged in the global marketplace as a quality-driven company which renders both interior design and building construction services, governed by work ethics and values.

We are oriented in maintaining client relationship via the utilization of creativity, integrity, consultancy and professionalism. Our objectives in this line of duty or services are to provide a reliable and measurable melioration in client’s building assets within the undermentioned standards:

Team work

  • Contemporary creative designs by our interior designers
  • Consultancy
  • Skills
  • Discovery and application of measurable design principles
  • Efficiency and effectiveness in planning process
  • Use of quality materials
  • Long – term investment and economic value.

Vision

To be an aesthetic and creative-driven company that inculcates a culture of beauty, quality and constructiveness among all social class in its dealings.

Our vision incorporates the provision of unique designs that suit all architectures or structures in the corporate, residential, markets and residential clients in Ghana and across West Africa. We endeavor to bring to our client’s contemporary and appealing designs to meet client’s requirements and budgetary needs. That is why RunBelows Ventures invest in educating and training its employees to meet daily and projected tasks as per clients demands or problem.

Value

To continually provide services under self and institutional – motivational measures that are seen in our working approaches, quality of materials used for our client’s projects, creativity, integrity, hard work, safety, respect, aesthetic and innovation. These values are adhered by both the managerial bodies and employees purposely to guarantee continual development, professionalism, survivability, and sustainability in this business venture. We believe that working closely with these values brings clients, consultants, contractors and suppliers the needed satisfaction. At RunBelows Ventures, we allow our client’s perspectives to meet our designs. This aspect is met with communication, efficacy, computational skills, creativity and hard work.


Culture

We believe in uniformity, securing and maintaining all values: integrity, safety, professionalism, creativity, aesthetic, hard work and innovation. These are exemplified in our management structures and everyday dealings with clients. We ensure a good interpersonal relationship with our clients with a communication plan attached to maintain them. The sustainable growth of our company is because of profound display and the pursuit of profitable ventures, nondiscrimination, succession planning and integrity.
